Dr. Allen is a Georgia Board Certified chiropractor. Dr. Allen was born in Natchez, Mississippi and raised in Zachary, Louisiana. He has lived in the Atlanta area since 1993.

Dr. Allen received his Masters of Clinical Pastoral Counseling from Revelation Message Bible College in Jacksonville, Florida. He is also recognized as being licenced with advanced standing with the National Christian Counseling Association (NCCA).

Dr. Allen's wife's name is Lynne. Lynne works in the office with Dr. Allen. Dr. Allen and Lynne were married in Baton Rouge, Louisiana and have been married since 1975.
